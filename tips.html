Useful Cooking Tips

Keep Your Knives Sharp

A sharp knife is safer and more effective than a dull one. Always keep your blades sharpened and store them properly.

Use Fresh Herbs

Fresh herbs like basil, parsley, and coriander bring more flavor and color to your dishes than dried ones.

Preheat Your Pan

Always heat your pan before adding ingredients. This helps with even cooking and prevents sticking.

Season as You Go

Add salt and spices during cooking, not just at the end. This helps the flavors build properly.

Let Meat Rest After Cooking

Before slicing meat, let it rest for a few minutes. This keeps it juicy and flavorful.
